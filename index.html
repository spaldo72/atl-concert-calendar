Apple Calendar

  1. Click Subscribe on Apple Calendar.
  2. Approve opening Calendar if asked.
  3. Tap Subscribe.
  4. Done.

Google Calendar

Google currently requires calendar subscriptions to be added from a desktop web browser.

  1. Open Google Calendar on your computer.
  2. Click the + beside Other Calendars.
  3. Select From URL.
  4. Paste the copied URL.
  5. Click Add Calendar.
